Reading

I read a lot — mostly fiction and some sci-fi. I’m into books that explain how things work, especially in science, history, or tech. Sometimes I’ll reread a passage just because it explained something in a really clean way.

Science & Space

I’m fascinated by how the universe works. Physics, astronomy, biology — it’s all connected. I don’t claim to know it all, but I like learning how things came to be, especially when it involves planets, stars, or life itself.

Programming

I spend time coding — mostly web stuff right now. I like figuring out how to build things from scratch or automate stuff just to see if I can. It's fun when something finally works after you debug it for an hour.

History

I'm not a historian, but I like understanding how people used to live and how events shaped the world. Sometimes I’ll fall into a rabbit hole reading about ancient civilizations or tech from 50 years ago that still matters today.
